Lasagna

Lasagna is the ultimate comfort food for someone looking for a blend of delicious cheese and pasta baked with a delicious sauce. Lasagna can be a time consuming meal to prepare, but the benefits of cooking a perfect homemade lasagna will pay off for up to three days of meals. Below you will find the ingredients list and step-by-step instructions to create a fabulous entree for you and the family.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
